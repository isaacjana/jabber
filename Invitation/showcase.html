Our Story

We met in 2017 at UITM Kota Samarahan 2 during an Annual General Meeting for the computer science faculty. Our first conversation started on Instagram, discussing our origins and interests. Our connection was instant, and despite a nervous first date and the challenges of a busy schedule and long-distance relationship, we grew closer and stronger together.

By early 2023, we knew we were ready to spend our lives together. Our proposal was simple and mutual, reflecting our deep understanding and compatibility. Today, our bond is stronger than ever, and our journey together has been the greatest adventure of our lives.

Schedule of Events

  • 6:30 PM - 6:45 PM - Guests Arrival and Seating

    Guests arrive and are guided to their seats. Background music by the live band.

  • 6:45 PM - 7:00 PM - Bride and Groom Walk-in

    Grand entrance of the bride and groom. Guests welcome the couple with applause. Live band plays an upbeat entrance song.

  • 7:00 PM - 7:15 PM - Couple Background Introduction

    Emcee or a close friend/family member introduces the bride and groom. Brief story of how the couple met, their journey, and engagement.

  • 7:15 PM - 8:00 PM - Dinner Service

    Dinner is served to the guests. Live band provides a soft musical background.

  • 8:00 PM - 8:15 PM - First Dance

    Bride and groom take the floor for their first dance. Live band plays the couple's chosen song.

  • 8:15 PM - 8:30 PM - Parent Dances

    Bride dances with her father, and groom dances with his mother. Live band plays selected songs for these dances.

  • 8:30 PM - 9:00 PM - Live Band Performance

    Live band performs a set of songs for guests to enjoy. Guests can join in dancing if they wish.

  • 9:00 PM - 9:15 PM - Cake Cutting

    Bride and groom cut the wedding cake. Emcee invites guests to gather around. Live band plays a celebratory tune.

  • 9:15 PM - 9:30 PM - Toasts and Speeches

    Best man, maid of honor, and parents give their toasts and speeches. Emcee introduces each speaker.

  • 9:30 PM - 10:00 PM - Open Dance Floor

    Guests are invited to dance. Live band plays a mix of songs to keep the energy up.

  • 10:00 PM - 10:15 PM - Last Dance

    Bride and groom share their last dance of the evening. Live band plays a special closing song.

  • 10:15 PM - 10:30 PM - Farewell and Send-Off

    Couple's farewell and thank you to the guests.

Wedding Color Theme

Our wedding reception will feature a beautiful palette of colors including Burnt Sienna, Pecan Pie, Dried Sage, Rosette, and Silk Scarf. These colors have been chosen to create a warm, elegant, and inviting atmosphere for our special day.
