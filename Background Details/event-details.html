Reception Tentative

6:30 PM - 7:00 PM: Welcome Reception

  • Guests arrive and mingle
  • Welcome drinks and light hors d'oeuvres served
  • Background music playing

7:00 PM - 7:15 PM: Couple Introductions

  • MC or host introduces the newlyweds
  • Newlyweds greet and thank the guests for attending

7:15 PM - 7:30 PM: Grand Entrance and Speeches

  • Bridal party and newlyweds make their grand entrance
  • Speeches or toasts may be given by the hosts, parents, or wedding party members

7:30 PM - 8:30 PM: Dinner Service

  • Guests are seated for dinner
  • Multi-course meal or buffet service
  • Background music continues

8:30 PM - 9:00 PM: Cake Cutting and Dessert

  • Cutting of the wedding cake
  • Dessert service begins
  • Coffee and tea served

9:00 PM - 9:30 PM: Photo Session

  • Newlyweds and guests participate in a photo session
  • Photographer captures group photos, candid shots, and couple portraits

9:30 PM - 10:30 PM: Dancing and Entertainment

  • DJ or band starts playing music
  • Open dance floor for guests
  • Traditional activities
  • Additional speeches or performances if planned

10:30 PM - 11:00 PM: Farewell and Thank You

  • Thank you message from the newlyweds
  • Guests bid farewell to the couple
  • Distribution of wedding favors, if any
